Research Journey

Timeline & Focus Areas

Key milestones in my research from wireless communications to secure AI-native networks.

2024 — Present

PhD Research: Secure FL for O-RAN

Developing differential privacy controllers for federated learning in Open RAN deployments. Three-controller comparison (Fixed, Heuristic, Yuan's Adaptive) with convergence analysis on BubbleRAN SDL datasets. Publication target: IEEE JSAC / TWC.

2023 — 2024

Space O-RAN & NTN Modeling

Built ns-3/ORAN-NTN simulation framework for LEO satellite networks. Implemented PHY-layer KPM extraction, E2SM-KPM for NTN, and federated learning across satellite constellations with ISL routing.

2022 — 2023

ISAC Waveforms & RIC xApps

Designed joint radar-communication waveforms for 6G ISAC. Developed xApps for Near-RT RIC: conflict management (5 resolution strategies), KPM monitoring, and RL-based resource allocation.

2021 — 2022

5G Core & OAI Integration

OpenAirInterface deployment on Pynq/USRP platforms. 5G SA core networking, gNB-CU/DU split, and RAN automation foundations.
